About Me

I am an analytics and insight leader with 18+ years of experience at Lloyds Banking Group, combining long-standing commercial and operational experience with increasingly technical analytical delivery. My career has developed across customer service, sales and retention, proposition development, commercial analysis, data insight, and analytics leadership.

Alongside full-time employment, I continue to study to strengthen the technical and theoretical foundations behind my work. My current areas of study include machine learning, mathematics for data science, visualisation, networks, and technology ethics. These subjects help me approach business problems with greater analytical rigour, broader systems understanding, and stronger awareness of responsible data use.

Professional Profile

My current work focuses on analytics leadership, stakeholder management, data product quality, strategic tooling direction, and insight delivery. I lead and support colleagues, help prioritise analytical workloads, challenge data products intended for business consumption, and contribute to the modernisation of tooling and infrastructure, including work connected to cloud-based data environments.
